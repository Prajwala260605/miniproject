TAMILNADU

INDIA SEASHELL MUSEUM

Visit the museum and behold the breathtaking collection of rare and unique shells - a veritable cornucopia of shapes, sizes, and colours - each more magnificent than the last. From the spiralled elegance of the conch shells to the delicate intricacy of cowrie shells, the museum's collection is an awe-inspiring visual feast.Embark on a journey to the Mamallapuram Seashell Museum, a unique and educational institution that celebrates the beauty and diversity of our ocean's bounty and offers an educational journey through the rich cultural heritage of this historic city. The Mamallapuram Seashell Museum is a repository of seashells and shells from across the world, presented in an artistic and enlightening manner. It aims to awaken the curious minds of visitors to the myriad uses of shells throughout history and the cultural significance they hold in various civilizations. In addition to the shells, the Mamallapuram Seashell Museum also sheds light on the history and significance of sea trade in Mamallapuram, once a hub for maritime commerce and a connector of ancient India with the rest of the world. Explore the role that seashells played in this trade and the cultural importance they held in different civilizations.
